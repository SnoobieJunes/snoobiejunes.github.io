The Gallows Way

A family card game of runs, wild cards, and nerve. Build runs of three or more same-suit cards, empty your hand, and race to 5,000 points. No two rounds are ever the same.

Download on the App Store For iPhone · Single player, local, and online multiplayer

🃏 Easy to learn

Think Uno meets Solitaire. A guided tutorial and in-app rules get new players going in minutes, with optional move hints on every turn.

🎲 Always fresh

Every round deals new wild cards and a new hand decided by a card flip — so strategy shifts game to game.

🏆 Compete worldwide

Global Game Center leaderboards track your career points and wins. See where you rank against players everywhere.

♿️ Built for everyone

Adjustable card and text sizes, high-contrast mode, a compact layout for small phones, and full VoiceOver support.

Four ways to play

📱
Single playerPass-and-play on one device, or test yourself against 1–3 computer opponents. Set every seat to AI to sit back and watch a full match.
📶
Local — WiFi & BluetoothPlay device-to-device with friends nearby, no internet needed. Perfect for camping, road trips, or anywhere service is spotty.
🌐
Online — Game CenterTurn-based matches at your own pace. Invite friends, auto-match with strangers, and get notified when it's your turn.
🔗
Challenge a friendSend a link and a friend can play a full match instantly through an App Clip — no purchase required to try it.
