Privacy Policy for The Gallows Way

Last Updated: May 29, 2026

The Gallows Way is developed and operated by Auston LeRoy.

Information We Collect

The Gallows Way does not directly collect, store, sell, or share personal information through its own servers.

Apple Services

The app uses Apple services, including Game Center, to provide multiplayer gameplay, matchmaking, invitations, turn-based notifications, achievements, leaderboards, and saved game synchronization.

Information processed through these services is managed by Apple and is subject to Apple's privacy practices. For more information, please visit Apple's Privacy Policy .

App Clips

The Gallows Way may offer an App Clip experience. Information processed through App Clips is handled through Apple's platform and subject to Apple's privacy practices.

Diagnostic Information and Crash Reports

Apple may collect diagnostic and crash information to improve the stability, reliability, and performance of the app. Such information is managed by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy practices.

Optional Bug Reports

Users may choose to submit bug reports by email to bugs@auston.org.

Bug reports may include diagnostic information such as application logs, game state information, device information, operating system version, and other technical details necessary to investigate and resolve software issues.

This information is used solely for troubleshooting, improving the app, and responding to support requests. It is not sold, shared for advertising purposes, or used for marketing.

Third-Party Services

The Gallows Way does not use third-party advertising networks, third-party analytics platforms, or marketing trackers.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
